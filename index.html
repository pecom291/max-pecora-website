Links and Descriptions

This is the homepage for the FFVII (Final Fantasy 7) about the character Cloud Strife. Each of the descriptions below have links to take you to the different pages and the navigation bar above can be used to do the same! Please Enjoy.

The Cloud Strife link will take you to the main character and protagonist of the video game known as Final Fantasy VII. He wields a rediculously large sword known as the buster sword which is basically a giant slab of metal with a handle and is known to be part of a military group called SOLIDER.

The Cloud's Rival link will take you to a page regarding Cloud's nemesis and rival Sephiroth. He is extrodinarily tall and a known war hero with a terrible upbringing. He wields and extremely large katana which is over several feet long and he is able to do it one handed.

The FAQ link will take you to a FAQ page for all of the most frequently asked questions about these characters!
